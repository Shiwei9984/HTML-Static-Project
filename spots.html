Things I don't like about NYC.

  • NYC Subway

    Even though train is convenient
    but I don't like it very much. It is very crowded.
    On the track, trash is everywhere and smell very bad sometime.
    Train is very slow sometime and got me almost late for several times.

  • Expense

    Everything in NYC is so expensive whether is food, cooking utensil, cloth, computer etc.
    My computer's price is around $1000 and the tax is like $150.
    Need to think before purchase any stuff.

    It is not also very far if I want to purchase anything.

  • Even though food is pretty expensive but I like the food here. Very diverse and delicious

  • I like the weather of NYC, not too cold or too hot for the most time.
    But I hate rainning, just hate to carry an umbrella.
